About

The OmniLoco Foundation is a non-profit foundation focused on UAVs. It was established in 2011 by David Carlson and Angus Peart. Tau Labs has decided to join forces and is governed by the OmniLoco Foundation which will handle all financial aspects of running the project and maintain control over all resources. The board of directors for the OmniLoco Foundation (as elected at the Dec 2012 annual board meeting) consists of: - Jose Barros - David Carlson - James Cotton - Vincent Kessler - Edouard Lafargue - Angus Peart - Dale Schinstock - Kenn Sebesta - Stacey Sheldon

Tau Labs

Tau Labs is an Open Source project aimed to create high quality code for controlling multirotors and other UAVs. Please see our about page for a long description of our goals objectives. For a list of features of our software, please see this page. The developement is driven entirely by volunteers donating their time in pursuit of this goal. Tau Labs also has an active community and we invite you to drop into our IRC channel (freenode.net #taulabs) or forums.
